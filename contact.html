Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a question not listed here?

Feel free to contact us directly using the form above or reach out via email or phone. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours during business days.

How do I register for TechCon 2024?

You can register directly through our registration page. Choose your ticket type, fill out the form, and complete your registration online.

What is the refund policy?

Refunds are available for cancellations made before December 10, 2024. For more details, please check our Terms and Conditions or contact our registration team.

Do you offer group registration discounts?

Yes! We offer special discounts for groups of 5 or more attendees. Please contact our registration team at register@techcon2024.com for details.

Is the conference accessible for people with disabilities?

Absolutely! We're committed to making TechCon inclusive and accessible. We offer accommodations including wheelchair accessibility, sign language interpreters, and captioning. Please indicate your needs during registration.

Can I volunteer at TechCon 2024?

We welcome volunteers! If you're interested in helping out, please select "Volunteer Opportunity" when submitting the contact form above.
